Intro

Enjoy the wildest threesome of video, card and miniature games, based on distributed ledger. Enter the world of permanent fights, primitive instincts, abuse of power and magic, wildest sex and impolite politics.

Unleash your darkest side where only not pussies survive the night.

The GAME

In 2176, 150 years after the Merge, a post-apocalyptic Earth is slowly and chaotically recovering from the disaster that buried 21 century human civilization, erupted planet’s ecosystem and brought new inhabitants.

Scattered across the ruins of past glory four races battle for survival and dominance: Orcs, Dwarves, Elves and the remains of Humans. Magic and sword is the new law in the land where travel, communication and even social constructs are diminished to the levels of Dark Ages.

In the fragmented world full of empty spaces, new forms of wildlife and unknown monsters, new saviors appeared – the Immortals, who fight each other in the magic-fused battles.
Take the role of the Immortal, pick 5 various characters to your warband and fight your rivals in highly competitive 1v1 matches or measure your powers in the story driven single player campaign.
Enjoy the game created both by passionate geeks and most advanced technologies. Distributed ledger perfectly solves the problem of in-game properties ownership. Artificial intelligence analyzes already played battles data and balances the game. It is also employed for practice mode to train you for real battles.

Elsewhere problems

Developer usurps in-game assets

In most cases, game terms of service forbid items, characters or accounts sharing and trading. All in-game assets legall­­­­­­­­­­y belong to the game developer. Legally speaking, even when players pay real money for in-game items they still don’t become real owners.

Dangers of the grey market

Nowadays players are forced to trade in-game assets in the grey market and in many occasions become victims of scammers and swindlers. Not mentioning that if they are caught by the game developer they get a life ban fortrading offsite.

Only a boredom of collecting

Distributed ledger is in fashion these days, so the technology is quickly gaining popularity in gaming industry, too. However, recent explosion of cryptopet games seems like a greedy misuse of decentralization possibilities. True geeks find mostf them only fruitless attempts without imagination, mainly using a single aspectof digital gaming - collecting.

Our solutions

You are the property owner

All Not for Pussies in-game items and characters are stored on distributedledger. Therefore, players maintain complete ownership of their assets and arefree to trade them as they wish. Players’ accounts also fully belong to them,so they can handle them as they please.

No risk for passionate gamers

Free trade based on distributed ledger solves all mentioned problems. Not for Pussies allows you to sell or buy assets inside the game. Legalization of trade eliminates risks for gamers and sweeps out the scammers.

Thrilling full-blooded game

Not for Pussies offers you a full-blooded highly competitive gamewith 3D models, animations and, most importantly, strong gameplay. A game withthe aspects geeks love in various games: card trading, miniature collecting, puzzles,objectives, trap avoidance, and a well written background story. Distributedledger also brings its benefits, because all the data becomes open, trusted anddecentralized.

Combine

Players get characters by combining 3 aspects: Class, Race and Element. For example, combining Rogue Class with Human Race and Wind Element the player gets Human Wind Rogue character as a result. Each aspect has its own min-max values which is randomized when combined, so each character has different stats and skill sets. Combining options are unlimited. You are able to combine already created characters and get even more powerful variations. Thus, the longer you play, the more options you have to create unique characters and new battle tactics.
